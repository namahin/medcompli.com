Federal Exclusion Databank

The MedCompli Exclusion & Sanction Screening service includes a review of the OIG List of Excluded Individuals/Entities (LEIE), GSA System for Award Management (GSA SAM), and other exclusions lists maintained by other governmental organizations such as the Department of Treasury, Department of State & Department of Commerce.

State Exclusion Databank

The MedCompli Exclusion & Sanction Screening service includes a review of the most up to date lists of individuals who have been terminated, suspended or placed on probation for actions constituting fraud, waste or abuse in the Medicaid program.
