The MedCompli Third Party Due Diligence Module simplifies the tasks of completing a risk based assessment of entities conducting business on your behalf. After entering in some basic demographic information on the third party an email with a link to a customized website can be forward automatically. This functionality can also be used to send annual reminders and is not limited to just OUS. As an example an annual email could be sent to all distributors referencing meal limits, transparency requirements, the AdvaMed Code of Ethics and other relevant topics. This is a great tool to communicate and document third party oversight.

Information on Third Party Sales and Marketing Intermediary (SMI) is entered into MedCompli

A personalized email with a link to a customized due diligence questionnaire is sent to the SMI.

The authorized official at the SMI will complete, certify and submit the questionnaire.

The completed questionnaire will be automatically populated in MedCompli for review and decision making.

Integration with the Transparency International Corruption Perception Index to assist in the risk based due diligence assessment.

Third Party Manager:

To minimize anti-corruption and anti-kickback risk it is critical for organizations to provide oversight of contracted third parties conducting business on your behalf. We can assist with policies, procedures, due diligence questionnaires and risk profile assessments.

MedCompli simplifies the process of communicating with and obtaining an annual compliance attestation, an important element in mitigating anti-corruption risk. Easily craft a custom email directing your distributors to the attestation. MedCompli will automatically track completion statistics and maintain documentation in the event of an audit. A reminder email and reports can be generated to streamline follow up.
