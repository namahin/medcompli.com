CompliSpend is expressly designed to assist companies in accurately reporting transfers of value made to healthcare professionals & teaching hospitals. We simplify the process of consolidating the needed data to ensure compliance with both federal and state transparency reporting mandates. Massachusetts, Connecticut, and Vermont databases & reporting are included for no additional fee.

Enter single transactions or upload data into our secure web-based portal.

Integration with the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES), CMS Federal Teaching Hospital List and the CMS OCR Validated Physician List.

Data validation algorithms and audit reports to proactively identify issues.

Integrated configurable spend expense threshold reports and sales force trend analysis with export, sort & filter capabilities.

Streamlined Dispute Resolution. Coordinate pre submission data dissemination to HCP’s to preemptively resolve discrepancies.

Expert advice and guidance to ensure a seamless process. We do 100 % of the work for many clients so that they can focus on other priorities. Let the MedCompli team worry about the data review, formatting and submission to CMS.

The software should adapt to your business, not make your business change to fit the software. We understand that requirement and have packed this application with all the pertinent features.
